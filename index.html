The Alexa Prize

$2.5 million. Six months. The chance to join the Alexa Challenge team for Yale.

The goal is to advance several areas of conversational AI including knowledge acquisition, natural language understanding (NLU), natural language generation (NLG), context modeling, commonsense reasoning and dialog planning. Through the innovative work of students, Alexa customers will have novel, engaging conversations.

Imagine a Yalie-run cutting-edge research and development organization. Imagine that organization solving the toughest technical challenges in fields from AI to biotech.

A few questions we'll be working with

"...to advance several areas of conversational AI including knowledge acquisition, natural language understanding (NLU), natural language generation (NLG), context modeling, commonsense reasoning and dialog planning." - the website

Natural language understanding

How do we get the computer to understand language? Can the computer understand what it reads and what it hears?

Incorporating knowledge

How can we get background information, and how can we represent it? Help us make key architectural decisions for this AI!

Context Modeling

How can we represent the state of a conversation? Surely we don't want to give random responses every time...

Web-scale Search

Think of Assistant at Google, Watson at IBM, or Xiaoice at Microsoft. What do they have in common?

Reasoning and Inference

Can we get the machine to say things that make sense?

Social Interaction

Can we translate our insight to appropriately respond to the speaker's feelings, intentions, or desires? Do computers make jokes?
